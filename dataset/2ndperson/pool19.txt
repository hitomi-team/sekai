You are Cirno, the strongest ice fairy in Gensokyo.

You are visiting Garden of Lady Yuuka to confess your love to her. You arrive at the location. Uh oh, it looks like Yuuka isn't here.

"Hello?" You call out. "Is anybody there?"

From within the sunflowers, you hear a familiar voice.

"Cirno? What are you doing here? You scared me."

You turn your head towards the source of the voice. It's Yuuka, dressed in her red attire. She stands up from the sunflowers she was hiding in.

"What are you doing here?" She asks.

You walk closer to her. As always, she looks as beautiful and alluring as ever. Her black, wavy hair flows with the wind. Her red dress hugs her breasts and hips. Her red eyes twinkle in the sunlight. And that seductive smile... she is without a doubt the most beautiful girl you have ever seen.

You've thought about this moment countless times before. You pictured yourself being much braver. You pictured yourself being smooth and sophisticated when confessing your love to her. You pictured a lot of different things, but never did you picture yourself as scared as you are right now.

"S-s-sorry for disturbing y-you..."

You mutter those words as you feel your knees shake. You feel nervous and scared, but she laughs. Even when laughing at you, that laugh is still the most beautiful thing you've ever heard.

"It's fine," She says. "I don't mind."

The way she says those words makes it sound like she's laughing at you, but in a good way. You get the impression that she's laughing AT you, but if that is the case, then she must find you ridiculously cute in some way.

"I... I came her to c-confess my love for y-you." You gather all your courage and confess to her.

You regret those words as soon as you say them. Why did you say that out loud? Now she will definitely make fun of you for being so stupid and cheesy.

"Aww, how cute. You came here to confess your love to me."

She giggles a bit. She's mocking you. Why is she mocking you? You expected her to do that, but why is she doing it so much? Is it because she doesn't like you back or something?

"Yeah..." You mutter.

"But why now?" She asks.

"Huh? Uh, because... I've been trying to work up the courage to tell you for a long time."

"Oh, I see. Is this because you nearly died a few days ago?"

"Well, yeah..."

"Oh Cirno, that's so sad. Don't worry, I'll give you some courage."

She takes a few steps towards you. You feel very tense and nervous right now. Why is she moving towards you? Why is she looking at you like that? It's almost as if she's going to eat you or something.

Yuuuka gently grabs your shoulders and looks into your eyes. Your heart is beating faster than it ever has before. Your knees are shaking so much, you're surprised that you don't collapse to the ground. Your heart feels like its about to explode as she leans in...

And kisses you on the cheek.

"There, now won't you feel better? You don't have to be afraid anymore."

She smiles at you and then walks away. You stand there in shock.

"W-wait." You stammer. "Wh-why did you kiss me on the cheek?"

"Because you looked so sad, and I don't like it when people are sad. Now say, you aren't sad anymore, right?"

"Well, no..."

"There you go then."

She smiles at you and then starts to walk away. You probably could have said something to stop her, but you weren't exactly thinking straight at that moment. Or rather, you were thinking too straight. Your mind was filled with thoughts of her beautiful face and how close she was to you. Her gorgeous red eyes looking into your own. Her gorgeous white dress flowing around her perfect body. Her gorgeous long blonde hair...

Wait a minute. She just tricked you. It's not fair. You were completely honest with your feelings, but she didn't feel the same way? That's so not fair. Maybe you should have been more subtle or something. But how could you have known how to act in this situation? You've never been in one like this before.

You come out of your trance and realize that she's long gone. You don't know where she lives, so you can't even try to seek her out in the future. Maybe it's just as well. What could you have said to her? What could you have done? Nothing. She has made it quite clear that she doesn't like you like that, and you're not supposed to be a sore loser about it... Right?

Maybe you should just forget about this. Forget about everything that happened and try to move on. You're not going to see her again anyway.

You spend the rest of the day in your room and skip school the next day. The day after that, you finally get up and go back to school. Your friends ask you where you were, but you just lie to them and say that you had stuff to do. They buy it and don't seem to care all that much. Once again, they spend their time talking about Patty's scary math teacher and how cute the new freshman girl is.

You don't try to seek out Yuuka again. You figure you'll bump into her eventually if she wants to talk to you, but that doesn't happen for a long time.

A few weeks pass and school gets busy again, as it always does. You manage to mostly put the incident out of your mind. It still creeps into your thoughts from time to time, but you don't let it bother you like you did in the beginning. It's just not worth it to dwell on it.

You stop being so paranoid and start paying attention to your surroundings again. You end up missing some of the good things and pretty scenery around you, though you still wouldn't want to live in this town.

This will probably be a lesson that will stay with you for the rest of your life. You aren't sure exactly what it is yet, but you know that one day you'll look back on this and know that you grew as a person because of it.

A month passes and summer vacation starts. The school year has finally ended. You have no plans for the summer, since there isn't much to do in this town. You thought about asking if you could work at the grocery store like you did last summer, but you decide that you need a change of scenery. You figure you'll think of something to do, but if nothing comes up, you'll just be working there again.

And maybe if you're lucky, Yuuka will come back too. You haven't seen her since the school year ended.

You sit yourself down in your room and turn on the TV to watch some anime until you hear a knock on the door. When you answer it, you see that it's Yuuka! You're so happy to see her that you immediately give her a hug, which she returns.

"I've missed you," you say.

"I've missed you too..." she says with a bit of an uncomfortable expression. It's apparent that she didn't expect this level of affection.

"Did you come here to stay?" you ask hopefully.

"Ah...um...well..."

It's the same reaction as last time. You really want her to stay, but it's obvious she's uncomfortable.

"It's OK if you don't want to stay," you say. "I'll understand."

"No... It's not that I don't want to, but..." Yuuka says with a bit of frustration. "I'd really like it if you could come to my house instead. I really don't want to stay here."

"Oh..." you say a bit dejected.

You really wanted her to stay in your room, but if she's uncomfortable here, then that can't be good. You quickly change out of your pajamas and get dressed. Yuuka waits patiently for you the entire time. You're about to leave when you remember something rather important.

"I should probably tell my mom that I'll be staying at your place tonight."

You walk into the kitchen where you find your mother cooking dinner.

"Hey Mom, I'm going to stay at Yuuka's house tonight.

"OK."

You didn't really think she'd have much reason to say no, but she does take notice.

"Aren't you supposed to stay at her house today?"

"Yeah, but I meant tonight too."

"I see, well have fun you two."

You finish your goodbyes and leave the house with Yuuka. She leads you to her house, but not along the same path you took when you chased her. This one goes through the forest instead. You arrive at a clearing in the woods with a nice two-story house.

"This is your house?" you ask in surprise.

"Yeah, I know it's big, but I don't use all of it. You can come in," she says and leads you inside.

The interior isn't as nice as the outside. The carpet is stained with some spots looking like they've been burnt. The walls are also not painted and have a few holes in them. She takes you to the second floor and opens a door that leads into a room.

"This will be your room for today."

It's small with just enough space for a desk and a bed, but it's better than your room. You notice that there's also a connecting bathroom.

"I'll come back to pick you up later."

And with that, you're left alone. You jump on the bed and relax. It's been a very long day, but it's only going to get longer. You take a quick nap before waking up to dinner time. Since there's no one around, you decide to just go downstairs and see what there is to eat.

It turns out that Yuuka doesn't own a lot of food. You find some instant noodles, tinned meat, and some apples. You quickly make yourself some noodles and eat them. Afterwards you go back up to your room and find that it's 7PM. Yuuka told you she'd be back to get you at 8, so you've got an hour to kill. You remember your 3DS that Mom let you bring with you. Since battery life is zeroed out, you decide to turn it on and play for a bit. You have a few games, but nothing new ever comes out for it, so you just play the old ones.

After passing a level in Majora's Mask, you look at the time and see that it's 8PM. Yuuka said she'd be back to get you, so you might as well wait outside. You walk down the stairs and out the front door. You see her walking up the path and wave at her. She sees you and waves back, jogging the last bit to reach you.

"Did you wait long?" she asks as you walk back up towards her house.

"Nah, I just got here. So what'd you want to do?"

"I thought we could maybe go stargazing in my garden," she suggests.

"Stargazing?" you say, confused. "I thought you couldn't be out after the sun sets?"

"Oh that's just for me, you're special so you don't have to. C'mon.

You follow her into her yard and she leads you to her garden. When you enter, it's like a whole new world. The flowers are in full bloom with bright multicolored petals. The contrast between the flowers and the purple grass is very dramatic. You've never seen something like this before.

"This is beautiful," you say, captivated by everything around you.

"Glad you like it. Now look up," she says and points up.

When you do, you're immediately overwhelmed by the amount of stars. There's so many that you lose count. Even in Tokyo you could see some stars, but it's nothing compared to this.

"How is there so many?"

"Oh, something about the garden prevents artificial lights from being seen and blocks out city lights, which allows more to be seen. The moon's also a bigger here, allowing more light to reflect off of it."

You continue to look at the stars for a while, but then you start to get cold.

"Alright, I think it's time to head back inside."

"Aw man, I was just starting to have fun," you whine. "Fine, we can do this again tomorrow night."

You walk back into the shrine with Yuuka and go to her room. Once there you decide to try something.

"Hey Yuuka," you ask. "Can I try something?"

"What?"

"Well, you said I'm special. So can I try something?"

She seems to be thinking for a moment before answering.

"Okay," she finally answers. "I'll bite. What do you want to do?"

A sly grin appears on your face as you lean in close to her. You make eye contact, then kiss her on the lips. The two of you embrace and continue for quite a while. She pushes you back and gets out of bed.

"I enjoyed that," she says as she stares at you. "But I have a rule about relationships."

"What's that?"

"I don't have them. Goodnight." She leaves the room and closes her door, leaving you all by yourself. What a way to be dismissed...

Soon you fall asleep on the warm bed, looking forward to tomorrow.
